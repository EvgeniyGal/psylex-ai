# PsyLex MVP – Main Page Requirements

## 1. General Description

The start page is the main landing interface of the PsyLex application. It is designed to communicate the core value proposition of AI-assisted conflict resolution and guide users into two primary modes:

* Self-resolution mode (Mode A)
* Mediator mode (Mode B)

The page must be visually minimal, high-contrast, and emotionally neutral (no aggressive legal aesthetics).

---

## 2. Hero Section (First Screen)

### Purpose

The hero section is the primary conversion element and must be fully visible without scrolling.

#### Main Headlines

**English:**

> Conflict doesn't need a winner. It needs a solution.

> «У спорі не повинно бути тих, хто програв»

---

* Secondary CTA:

#### Visual Element

A single animated metaphor must be selected:

* Prism OR
* Bridge OR
* Two rivers merging

Design requirements:

* Dark navy background
* Light glow at the point of convergence
* Minimal, calm animation
* Symbolizes transformation of conflict into agreement

## 5. How It Works Section

### Purpose

Explain workflows for both modes.

---

### Mode A (Self-resolution)

1. Describe your conflict and complete a short test
2. AI builds a profile and starts a structured dialogue
3. Receive a resolution plan to propose to the other side

---

### Mode B (Mediator)

1. Mediator completes a test and adds both parties
2. AI analyzes psychological profiles of all three participants and highlights risks
3. Mediator conducts session with AI support and receives a report
