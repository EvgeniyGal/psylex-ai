    Закон — спільне джерело (EN, дослівно)

    Before an HOA may impose a fine, the owner must receive 14 days' written notice and a hearing before an independent committee of at least three members who are not board members, employees, or their relatives. The committee — not the board — approves the fine. A fine issued without this process may be open to challenge. Fines are generally capped at $100 per violation unless the governing documents allow more.
